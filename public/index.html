Welcome to RunMate

RunMate allows you to track your running activities and moniter your progress as you go. Record your runs and view a breakdown of your fitness journey.

INTENSITY

As the intensity of your workouts increases over time, it indicates improvements in cardiovascular fitness and endurance.

DISTANCE

The distance you run indicates your ability to cover more ground and potentially increase stamina and endurance.

CALORIES

Calories burned during your run can be indicative of the energy expenditure and metabolic impact of the exercise. Download RunMate on your Fitness Tracker to gauge the intensity and efficiency of your workouts.
